OCCUPATIONAL SAFETY CHECKLIST
=============================

1. GENERAL WORK ENVIRONMENT
[ ] Are all work areas clean, sanitary, and orderly?
[ ] Are aisles and passageways kept clear?
[ ] Are wet surfaces covered with non-slip materials?

2. FIRE SAFETY
[ ] Are fire extinguishers accessible and charged?
[ ] Are emergency exits marked and unblocked?
[ ] Are employees trained in fire safety procedures?

3. ELECTRICAL SAFETY
[ ] Are all electrical cords in good condition (no fraying)?
[ ] Are circuits properly labeled?
[ ] Is lockout/tagout used for machinery maintenance?

4. PPE (PERSONAL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT)
[ ] Is required PPE available (helmets, gloves, glasses)?
[ ] Is PPE properly maintained and stored?
[ ] Are employees trained on how to use PPE?

This is a sample checklist provided by Theuri Green Health Safe.
