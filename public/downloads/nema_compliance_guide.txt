NEMA COMPLIANCE OVERVIEW (2026)
===============================

IMPORTANT REGULATIONS SUMMARY

1. ENVIRONMENTAL IMPACT ASSESSMENT (EIA)
   - All new projects must undergo an EIA before commencement.
   - A license must be obtained from NEMA.

2. WASTE MANAGEMENT
   - Segregation of waste at source is mandatory.
   - Hazardous waste must be handled by licensed handlers.
   - "Polluter Pays" principle applies.

3. WATER QUALITY
   - Effluent discharge into environment must meet quality standards.
   - Regular monitoring and reporting is required.

4. NOISE CONTROL
   - Noise levels must be within permissible limits.
   - Construction noise permits required for night work.

Visit https://www.nema.go.ke for the full Act and regulations.
